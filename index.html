How I Work

My 3-Step Content Action Process

Every brand I work with goes through this exact framework — no guesswork, no wasted posts. Just a clear path from brand identity to final content.

Step One · Foundation

Brand Identity Chart

Before a single caption is written, I map the full brand identity — who they are, what they stand for, and how they should sound. This becomes the brand's north star for every content decision.

🎨
Brand Voice & Tone Profile
Defining the personality: playful, professional, bold, warm — or a custom blend.
🎯
Target Audience Persona Map
Age, platform habits, pain points, what content makes them stop scrolling.
💡
Core Values & Messaging Pillars
3–5 content themes the brand will own and repeat consistently.
🖼️
Visual Identity Guidelines
Colour mood, typography feel, and aesthetic direction for all visuals.
1
2
Step Two · Intelligence

Niche Research & Content Planning

With a solid identity in hand, I go deep into the competitive landscape, trending formats, and platform algorithms. Then I build a content plan that fills every gap and exploits every opportunity.

🔍
Competitor & Niche Audit
Analysing what's working, what's missing, and where the gap is for this brand.
📊
Platform & Trend Research
Instagram, LinkedIn, TikTok, X — each platform gets its own format strategy.
🗓️
Editorial Content Calendar
A 4–8 week posting schedule with content types, topics, and publishing cadence.
📈
KPI Framework Setup
Defining what success looks like — reach, saves, engagement rate, or conversions.
Step Three · Execution

Scripting & Final Content Ideas

Everything from steps 1 and 2 feeds into this — ready-to-use scripts, captions, hooks, and post frameworks that are on-brand, platform-optimised, and built to perform.

✍️
Post Scripts & Captions
Full copy for reels, carousels, threads, and static posts — ready to publish or record.
🎬
Hook Writing & Video Briefs
First-3-second hooks for short-form video, with full scene-by-scene briefs.
🧠
Content Idea Bank (10–20 ideas)
A reusable library of brand-specific ideas derived from research — never generic.
🔄
Repurposing Strategy
One idea → 5 formats. Maximising every asset across all platforms.
